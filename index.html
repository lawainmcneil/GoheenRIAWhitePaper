Frequently Asked Questions

What RIAs Ask Us Most

What is premium finance, in plain terms?

Premium finance allows UHNW individuals to secure large life insurance policies using borrowed funds — keeping their invested assets fully deployed rather than liquidating them to pay premiums. It’s a capital efficiency strategy, not an insurance play.

How does partnering with Goheen affect my client relationship?

It strengthens it. Goheen operates as your invisible structuring desk. All client-facing materials are advisor-branded. Your client sees you as the strategist; we handle the engineering behind the scenes. You never lose control of the relationship.

What size clients benefit most from this strategy?

The Liquidity Preservation Strategy is designed for estates exceeding $30M in net worth where estate tax obligations, asset concentration, or illiquidity create a need for structured estate leverage. That said, every situation is unique and we’re happy to evaluate individual cases.

How does Goheen make money? Is there a cost to the RIA?

Goheen is compensated through standard insurance carrier commissions. There is no additional fee to the RIA or the client for our structuring and design work. The economics are fully aligned with your client’s best outcome.

I’m not an insurance expert. Can I still partner with Goheen?

Absolutely — that’s exactly the point. You don’t need to be an insurance expert. You need to be a great advisor. Goheen handles all of the technical structuring, underwriting coordination, and lender negotiation. You present the strategy. We make it work.

What makes Goheen different from SCA or Lionsmark?

Three things: speed (24-hour case design vs. 72+ hours), institutional backing (Simplicity Group’s national network), and the advisor-first gatekeeper model. We don’t compete with you for the client relationship — we power it from behind the scenes.
